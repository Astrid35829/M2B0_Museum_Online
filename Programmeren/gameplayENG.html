The Barcade

Welcome to our Barcade, where modern catering and retro arcade cabinets come together in a cozy environment.
Here you can refuel after hours of adventuring, challenges and saving the world.
In addition to the well-known halal hot dogs and sandwiches, we now also offer healthy sandwiches.
The Barcade is also open to the public who do not wish to visit the museum, so bring your judo teacher, your neighbor or your grandmother for a nice cup of coffee!
