History

The NVGM is the place where fun and learning come together.
Come to the museum to be taken through history by one of our museum teachers of this unique cultural heritage.
You will learn about the development of video games from entertainment to social relevance and current use in education.

Are you working on a certain theme at school and you don't know whether NVGM fits in well with this?
Would you like to discuss this with an educator in the museum? A lot is possible!

Tailoring a visit also encourages us to look at our diverse collection with different eyes.

In any case, you can count on one and a half hours in which you can play all the video games in the museum as many times as you want.

This also inspires us, so please mail to:
info@nationaalvideogamemuseum.nl
to get in touch come with our educator.
